## Rationale for term selection

The equation combines a broad anchor term (`"ethnomathematics"`) with
more specific expressions targeting the scope of this review: methodological
approaches (`"ethnomathematical methods"`), research synthesis
(`"systematic literature review about ethnomathematics"`,
`"bibliometric analysis on ethnomathematics"`), theoretical orientations
(`"culture and ethnomathematics"`, `"local mathematical knowledge"`,
`"indigenous mathematical knowledge"`), and research gap framing
(`"trends of ethnomathematics"`, `"gaps of ethnomathematics"`,
`"challenges of ethnomathematics"`, `"future research of ethnomathematics"`).

The `TITLE-ABS-KEY` restriction ensures that retrieved documents treat
ethnomathematics as a central focus rather than a tangential reference,
reducing noise relative to full-text search approaches.
